About Us

Started in May 2018, MyLrl.in is an Indore based start-up; the founders and team have put 8+ of experience to introduce you with only one of its kind system in transport industry for making online LR, Challans, voucher, Invoice, finance, POD management and online transporter search engine.

The beauty of MyLr is, it’s the only platform where you can create all document complies in your transport business along with accounting and reports.

MISSION

To digitize the transporters activities of doing business, by providing this simple platform so that each element (GTA, parcel booking service, transport broker or fleet owner) of the industry can be a part of future digitalized transport industry.

VISION

We want to be the leading platform in transport industry to digitalize all most all actions of a user. That improves the quality, efficiency and effectiveness in order to grow in total.
